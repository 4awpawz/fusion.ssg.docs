Why fusion.ssg?

Philosophy

fusion.ssg creates HTML documents and gets out of the way when you are using it.

Minimal

fusion.ssg is built from the ground up to be just that.

Familiar

Who said familiarity breeds content? fusion.ssg employs concepts you are already familiar with, like templates, markdown, tokens, Typescript components, .etc.

Productive

No complicated tooling and configuration. fusion.ssg compiles components and executes them in its sandboxed environment.

fusion.ssg's starter project should be all that's needed to get up and running quickly.
